# Election_Analysis
## Overview
This audit analysis was performed to help break down the results of a recent congressional race.
## Results
- 369,711 votes were cast in the election
- The precinct includes three counties, shown here with percentage of votes and total votes:
   - Jefferson: 10.5%, 38,855
   - Denver:    82.8%, 306,055
   - Arapahoe:  6.7%, 24,801 
- Denver County had the largest turnout by far, accounting for nearly five times the number of votes from the other two counties combined
- The following three candidates were up for election, shown here with percentage of total votes and number of votes recieved:
   - Charles Casper Stockham: 23.0%, 85,213
   - Diana DeGette:           73.8%, 272,892
   - Raymon Anthony Doane:    3.1%, 11,606
- Diana DeGette won in a landslide, amassing 272,892 votes, good for 73.8% of all total votes
## Election-Audit Summary
This script can be used in any election, provided modifications are made depending on the level of the election (city, state, country, etc) and the type of information the board is looking to retrieve.
